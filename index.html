About us

The Secret of the Silk Sea 🌊 🖋️

Before it was a brand, Royalanci was a dream written in the margins of a notebook.

The story begins with a girl who felt like a traveler in the wrong century. In a world of fast fashion and fleeting promises, she craved the Old-World—the weight of a wax seal on a letter, the loyalty of a lifelong companion, and the scent of royalty.

Royalanci is where the sophisticated artistry of French Grasse meets the soulful, ancient traditions of Middle Eastern Attars. We have bridged the gap between the West’s delicate elegance and the East’s mysterious depth, creating a scent profile that is as complex as a classic.
